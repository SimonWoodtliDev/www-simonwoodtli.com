About Me

I was interested in computers and technology from a very early age. The very first computer we had came from a friend of my mom's and was an aging machine that ran under MS-DOS. At that time I knew how to insert disks and play games like Indiana Jones. However, my dad showed me a few basic commands to navigate through the system. But for me it was just a huge playground to experiment.

A few years later, my father bought us a Windows 98 machine that we built from scratch. When we were done, it felt like magic to me because the GUI was so much easier to navigate. I soon became the IT department of family and friends to solve computer-related problems.
